Out of the Shell

Welcome to the “Out of the Shell” social page. President Savannah Ellison and Vice President Jon Yahr confounded this club in 2015 to help students that are struggling with the transition from high school to college find new friends. This club was initially designed to help underclassman students, but we have had plenty of seniors join and have positive experiences. Our executives plan weekly events, which often include: picnics, grabbing pizza, watching movies, game nights, hiking, and much more. We find that members who attend the club for a month sometimes leave since they find a good group of friends through our club. That being said, you are always welcome to continue hanging out with the friendly people at the “Out of the Shell” club.
